VISION

Daffodil Public School intends to join India’s development journey by
creating valuable human resources & the future of tomorrow by investing
in "Our Child".

MESSAGE

Principal

Daffodil Public School provides a challenging & supportive environment for the
best possible programme of academic & personal development as mentioned in the
National Education Policy (NEP 2020). The school is committed to building the
child’s confidence & self-esteem and offers a strong developmental programme
where integrated studies & hands-on participation inspire a love of learning
and accomplishment.

The school also encourages self-discipline, creativity, independence of thought,
and tenacity of purpose with the understanding that both success & failure
are necessary for learning.

We intend to develop social awareness by honouring all religions & promoting
commitment to the community & nation.
