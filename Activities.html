♦ YOGA 🧘

Yoga is a practice that unites body and mind,
and for student, it improves concentration,
reduces stress, and enhances physical flexibility
and strength. By incorporating yoga,
students can achieve a greater sense of calm and focus,
which helps them both in their studies and in life.

♦ Running/Jogging 🏃 

            • Boost your health and mood with every stride.
            Running is a simple, effective way to improve cardiovascular fitness,
            build strong bones, and reduce stress.

            •  A powerful path to a longer, healthier life.
            Just 5 to 10 minutes of running a day can significantly lower your

♦ Meditation 🧘 
            • Meditation is a simple yet profound practice
            of training your attention and awareness to achieve a state of mental
            calm and clarity. By focusing on a specific anchor, such as your breath
            or a mantra, you learn to observe your thoughts and feelings without
            judgment, gently guiding your mind back to the present moment whenever
            it wanders. This process helps clear away mental clutter and information
            overload, which can significantly reduce stress, anxiety, and negative
            feelings.
